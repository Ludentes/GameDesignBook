Hearthfire GM Reference

Position (How Risky?)

Set this BEFORE the player rolls. Say it out loud.

PositionOn FailureOn Partial
SafeNothing bad, just don't succeedMinor inconvenience
RiskyModerate consequence (harm, complication, lost opportunity)Choose: reduced effect OR consequence
DangerousSevere consequence (serious harm, major setback)BOTH consequence AND reduced effect

Default is Risky. Use Safe when they have clear advantage. Use Dangerous when they're outmatched or reckless.

Effect (How Much?)

EffectOn Full Success
LimitedProgress, but don't fully resolve
StandardAchieve intent as stated
GreatAchieve intent plus extra benefit

Default is Standard. Limited for tough obstacles or poor positioning. Great when stars align.

Threat Dial (Combat)

ThreatPositionEnemy StressPurpose
TrivialSafe2 eachShow competence
ChallengingRisky4 eachExciting, some cost
DangerousRisky, enemies first6 eachTense, real risk
DesperateDangerous8+ eachSurvival is victory

Damage dealt:

  • Full success = 2 stress
  • Partial = 1 stress
  • Great effect = +1, Limited = -1

Scene Pacing

Target: 4-6 scenes per session

Scene TypeDurationNotes
Action10-15 minCombat, chase—keep momentum
Social10-15 minNPCs have wants
Discovery10-15 minPlant clues
Character5-10 minIntimate, spotlight one
Transition3-5 minMontage it

If scene passes 15 minutes: Look for resolution. If scene passes 20 minutes: Actively wrap it.

PhaseTimeWhat Happens

NPC Quick-Build

Name: _______________

Role: What they do (guard, merchant, informant)

Want: What they're after right now

Quirk: One memorable detail

If combat: Threat level → stress boxes

Situation Builder

Status Quo: What's happening when players arrive

Pressure: Why it can't stay static

Stakes: What's at risk / what's available

Complications (pick if needed):

  • Reinforcements arrive
  • Time pressure
  • Innocents at risk
  • Hidden agenda revealed
  • Moral dilemma
  • Environment hazard
  • Unexpected ally
  • Stakes escalate

Compels

When a player's aspect would cause them trouble:

"Your 'Can't Walk Away From a Fight' means you can't just let this insult slide... right?"

If they accept: Give them 1 Fate Point, the trouble happens. If they refuse: They pay 1 Fate Point to resist.

Good compels create drama, not punishment.

Spotlight Check

Mental checklist—have all players:

  • □ Had a moment this session?
  • □ Used their character's strengths?
  • □ Made a meaningful choice?

If someone's quiet, frame a scene at them:

"Saren, the merchant looks right at you. 'I know your face. You're the one who...' What does he know?"
