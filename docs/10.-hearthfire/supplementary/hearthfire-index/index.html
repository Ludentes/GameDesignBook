Hearthfire: Supplementary Materials

Everything you need to play.

What's Included

For Players

Quick Rules — The complete player-facing rules on one page. Print this for each player.

Character Card Template — Blank character card, front and back. Print on cardstock or index cards.

Character Creation Guide — Step-by-step instructions for making a character from scratch.

Pre-Generated Characters — Six ready-to-play heroes. Pick one, fill in the blanks, and go.

Heroic Abilities — Complete list of special abilities to choose from, plus guidelines for creating your own.

For the GM

GM Reference Sheet — Position/Effect, Threat Dial, scene pacing, NPC building, session structure. Everything at a glance.

Session 1 Guide — How to run your first session, step by step.

The Millhaven Situation — A complete starting scenario with NPCs, locations, a four-session arc outline, and session 1 structure.

Example of Play — A complete scene showing how the game flows, with annotations explaining what's happening.

Quick Start Checklist

Before Session 1:

  • Read the Quick Rules
  • Read the GM Reference
  • Read the Session 1 Guide
  • Read the Millhaven Situation (or prep your own)
  • Print character cards (blank or pre-gens)
  • Print player Quick Rules (one per player)
  • Print GM Reference (one copy)
  • Gather Fate Point tokens (coins, beads, poker chips)
  • Bring paper for session record

At the Table:

  • Distribute character cards
  • Distribute Fate Point tokens (2 per player)
  • Have GM Reference visible
  • Have dice ready (at least 5d6)

Print Guide

What to print:

DocumentCopiesNotes
Quick Rules1 per playerCan share if needed
Character Card1 per playerCardstock recommended
GM Reference1Keep visible during play
Pre-gens (optional)1 setIf not doing full character creation

What not to print:

  • Character Creation Guide (reference during creation, not during play)
  • Session 1 Guide (GM reads beforehand)
  • Millhaven Situation (GM reads beforehand)
  • Example of Play (teaching tool, not play aid)
  • Heroic Abilities (reference during creation)

The Hearthfire Promise

This game is designed for:

  • Busy adults who can only meet monthly
  • 2-hour sessions that feel complete
  • 4-6 week gaps that don't kill the story
  • Minimal prep for players and GM
  • Rules that disappear during play

If the system ever gets in the way of the story, trust the fiction and move on.
